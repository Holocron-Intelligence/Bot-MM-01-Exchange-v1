# 🏁 Getting Started

Setting up ZeroOne is designed to be seamless. Follow this guide to launch your engine in under 5 minutes.

---

## 1️⃣ Open your Variant
ZeroOne comes in three specialized versions. Choose the folder that matches your goal:
*   `aaaaaaaaa`: **Stable Version** (Balanced)
*   `aaaaaaaaa_optimized`: **Optimized BE** (Capital Protection Focus)
*   `aaaaaaaaa_hyper_volume`: **Hyper Volume** (Maximum Points Focus)

## 2️⃣ Launch the Interface
Inside your chosen folder, find and double-click the **`launcher.py`**. 
> [!TIP]
> If you prefer a standalone experience, use the `.exe` file inside the `dist` folder.

## 3️⃣ Configuration
Once the launcher opens, fill in your details:
*   **Exchange ID (UID)**: Your unique identifier from 01 Exchange.
*   **Operational Capital**: The amount of USD the bot should use to calculate order sizes.
*   **Market Selection**: Tick the boxes for the coins you want to monitor.

## 4️⃣ Initialize & Monitor
Click the green **INITIALIZE BOT (AVVIA)** button. 

**What happens next?**
1.  The Bot Engine starts in the background.
2.  A **Live Dashboard** opens automatically in your browser.
3.  You can watch every trade, fill, and volume milestone in real-time.

---

### Pro Tips for New Users:
*   **Start in Paper Mode**: Tick the "Paper trading" box first to watch the bot trade with virtual funds.
*   **Check the Logs**: If the dashboard doesn't show movement, check the `logs/` folder for technical details.
*   **Stay Stable**: Ensure your computer has a stable internet connection for high-frequency requoting.
