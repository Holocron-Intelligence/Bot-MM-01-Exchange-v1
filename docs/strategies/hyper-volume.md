# ⚡ Hyper Volume

The **Hyper Volume** variant is the "Sword" of ZeroOne. It is an aggressive, high-speed engine designed for one thing: **Dominating the 01 Exchange leaderboard.**

---

## ⚡ Maximum Point Generation

Hyper Volume doesn't wait for the perfect entry. It creates frequency. By staying hyper-close to the market price (mid-price), it ensures maximum fill rates.

### Key Performance Features:

### 1. Ultra-Tight Spreads
This version quotes with minimal buffers. It aims to be the "top of the book" at all times, ensuring that almost every market move results in a fill for your bot.

### 2. High-Speed Scalping
Instead of holding positions for minutes, Hyper Volume looks for "sub-minute" exits. It flips positions rapidly, turning over your capital dozens of times per hour.

### 3. Volume Multiplier
Due to the sheer number of trades, this version can generate **10x to 20x** more volume than the Stable version with the same capital.

---

## ⚠️ Important Considerations
*   **Higher Frequency = Higher Risk**: Because the spreads are tighter, the bot is more exposed to market direction.
*   **Solana Fees**: Ensure you have enough SOL in your wallet, as the high number of transactions will consume network fees faster.
*   **Best in "Chop"**: This bot thrives in sideways, "choppy" markets where it can flip positions continuously.

---

### Configuration Recommendation:
*   **Order Size**: 50-60%
*   **Timeframe**: 1m (Required for max frequency)
*   **Markets**: Focus on the top 3-4 most liquid pairs for extreme throughput.
